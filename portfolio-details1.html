The main objective of the system is to manage the details of widows,volunteers and donations . This is only accessed by the admin. So the confidentiality of the data is maintained.The main objective of this system is to manage details of the rooms such as which room is activated and available for a member(widow) to use. The admin can add new members(widows) and also allocate them rooms only which are activated and available for use. He must add their basic information and also add if they want any training so they can look for jobs. He can also update and delete their details.
